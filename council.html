DEPARTMENT COUNCIL

Department of Geography, Kirori Mal College

In each academic year, the students of the department contest elections to be member of the council. The number of positions are fixly distributed among all the 4 years, in order to ensure participation from each year.

The distribution of position is Association Advisor (4th year), President (3rd year), Vice-president (3rd year), General Secretary (2nd year), Treasurer (2nd year), Junior Vice President (1st year) and Joint Secretary (1st year).
